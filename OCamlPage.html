OCaml Gambit

Daniel Lines, Jock Li, Atuyla Lohani, Emily Wang

Project Overview

As part of CS 3110, I collaborated with a team to develop a feature-rich chess game in OCaml, complete with multiplayer functionality and accurate detection of check and checkmate states. Our team wrote over 1,500 lines of code and utilized Bisect to achieve over 90% code coverage. Throughout the project, we regularly met with our project coordinator, ensuring that out timeline and project met expectations.
